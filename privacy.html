Privacy Policy

Data Storage

Your order data is securely stored in Google Sheets. We use Google Apps Script for order processing and Firebase Realtime Database for live updates.

Notifications

Our admin team receives instant notifications via Email and Telegram when new orders are placed. This ensures quick processing of your requests.

Third-Party Services

We integrate with SocialFastMM for SMM services and use Firebase for realtime data synchronization. No third-party tracking or analytics are used.

Contact Information

For any privacy concerns or data removal requests, please contact us at:
Email: support@easyrecharge.mm
Telegram: @EasyRechargeMM

Data Retention

Order data is retained for 6 months for accounting and support purposes. You can request data deletion at any time by contacting support.

Security

All data transmissions are secured using HTTPS. Payment information is never stored on our servers - we only store transaction IDs for verification.
