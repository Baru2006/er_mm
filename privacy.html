Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2024

Data Collection and Storage

At EasyRecharge MM, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. Here's how we handle your data:

Information We Collect

  • Order Information: Service details, phone numbers, game IDs, transaction IDs
  • Payment Information: Payment method, transaction amounts (we do not store full payment details)
  • Technical Information: Device type, browser information, IP address
  • User Identification: Unique user ID generated for order tracking

Data Storage

Your data is securely stored in the following systems:

Google Sheets

All order information is stored in secure Google Sheets with restricted access. This includes:

  • Order details and timestamps
  • Service specifications
  • Payment information
  • Order status updates

Firebase Realtime Database

For real-time order tracking and status updates, we use Firebase Realtime Database with the following security measures:

  • Data is stored in lockmode (read-only for users)
  • Encrypted data transmission
  • Regular security updates

Notifications and Communications

Admin Notifications

When you place an order, our admin team receives notifications through:

  • Email: Immediate email notifications for new orders
  • Telegram: Real-time Telegram messages with order details

Customer Communications

We may contact you regarding your orders through:

  • Order status updates via the platform
  • Support communications when you contact us
  • Service announcements and updates

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to provide our platform:

  • Google Apps Script: Backend processing and automation
  • Firebase: Real-time database for order tracking
  • SocialFastMM API: SMM service integration
  • Payment Providers: Wave, KBZPay, AYA, CB Pay for payment processing

Important: We do not use any third-party tracking services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks that compromise your privacy.

Data Retention and Security

Retention Period

  • Order data: 2 years for accounting and support purposes
  • User activity logs: 6 months
  • Payment transaction records: As required by financial regulations

Security Measures

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS)
  • Secure server infrastructure
  • Regular security audits
  • Limited access to sensitive information

Contact Information

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us:

  • Support Email: support@easyrecharge.mm
  • Telegram Support: @EasyRechargeMM_Support
  • Business Hours: 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM (Myanmar Time)

We are committed to responding to privacy-related inquiries within 24 hours.

Note: This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. We will notify users of any significant changes through our platform.
