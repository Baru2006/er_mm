Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how EasyRecharge MM ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our service.

1. Information We Collect

  • User ID: We generate and store a random, anonymous User ID in your browser's Local Storage to identify your orders. We do not collect personal names or emails.
  • Order Details: We collect information necessary to fulfill your order, such as target phone numbers, game IDs, or social media links.
  • Technical Data: We may store your theme preference (light/dark mode) in Local Storage. We do not use tracking cookies.

2. How We Use Information

Your information is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To process and fulfill your orders.
  • To provide you with order status updates.
  • To offer customer support if you contact us.
  • To improve our service and prevent fraud.

3. Data Storage and Security

  • Your order data is stored securely in a private Google Sheet.
  • Access to this data is restricted to authorized administrative personnel only.
  • We do not share, sell, or rent your personal information to any third parties.

4. Local Storage

Our service uses your browser's Local Storage to store your anonymous User ID and theme preference. This is necessary for the website to function correctly. This data is stored only on your device and is not a "cookie."

5. Policy Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Contact Information

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: support@easyrecharge.mm
