Frequently Asked Questions

Placing an order is simple:
  1. Go to the 'Order Form' page.
  2. Choose your service category and specific service.
  3. Provide the required info (like Phone Number or Game ID).
  4. Select your payment method (Wave, KBZPay, etc.).
  5. Pay the total amount to the account number shown.
  6. Enter your payment Transaction ID in the form and submit.
Delivery times vary by service:
  • SIM/Data: Usually instant (1-2 minutes).
  • Game TopUp: 1-5 minutes, depending on the game server.
  • SMM Services: 24-72 hours to start and complete.
You can track your order status on the 'Live Status' page.
We currently accept all major local payment wallets:
  • Wave
  • KBZPay
  • AYA
  • CBPay
