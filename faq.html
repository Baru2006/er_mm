Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

To place an order:

  1. Go to the Orders section from the main menu
  2. Select your service category (SIM/Data, Game TopUp, or SMM)
  3. Fill in the required information
  4. Choose your payment method and complete the payment
  5. Enter your transaction ID and submit the order

Delivery times vary by service:

  • SIM/Data: 5-15 minutes
  • Game TopUp: 1-10 minutes
  • SMM Services: 15 minutes to 24 hours (depending on quantity)
  • P2P Exchange: 5-15 minutes

We accept all major Myanmar payment methods:

  • Wave Money
  • KBZPay
  • AYA Pay
  • CB Pay

You can track your order status in real-time:

  1. Go to the Status section from the main menu
  2. Select the appropriate tab (Your Orders, Order Status, P2P Status, or SMM Status)
  3. View your order details and current status
  4. Use the refresh button to get the latest updates

If your order fails:

  1. Check your transaction ID is correct
  2. Wait 15 minutes and check the status again
  3. If still failed, contact our support team
  4. Provide your order ID and transaction details
  5. We will investigate and process refunds if necessary

You can contact our customer support through:

  • Email: support@easyrecharge.mm
  • Telegram: @EasyRechargeMM_Support
  • Phone: 09-123 456 789 (9AM - 6PM)

Please have your order ID ready when contacting support.
