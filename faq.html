FAQ & Help

How do I place an order?

Choose a service (SIM, Game, SMM or P2P) from the dashboard and fill the form. Ensure required fields are filled and submit — you'll get a confirmation and an email notification.

What payment methods are supported?

We support KBZPay, WavePay and bank transfers. Select the payment method in the form and provide the transaction ID where required.

How do role-based prices work?

On first visit a persistent UserID and Role (Customer default) are stored in your browser. Resellers see discounted pricing automatically.

How to track orders?

Go to Order Status and refresh. Orders are pulled from the backend Google Sheet and display status, totals and timestamps.

Contact & Support

Use the Admin Phone copy box in forms to copy the admin contact number for quick payment transfers.
