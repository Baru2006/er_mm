Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

To place an order:

  1. Go to "Orders" in the bottom navigation
  2. Select your service category (SIM/Data, Game TopUp, or SMM)
  3. Fill in the required details
  4. Choose your payment method
  5. Make payment to the provided number
  6. Enter the transaction ID and submit

We accept the following payment methods:

  • KBZ Pay
  • Wave Money
  • CB Pay

Make sure to use the correct payment number shown in the order form and always include your transaction ID.

Delivery times vary by service:

  • SIM/Data: Instant to 5 minutes
  • Game TopUp: 5-15 minutes
  • SMM Services: 1-24 hours (gradual delivery)
  • P2P Exchange: 5-15 minutes

If your order is delayed, please check the order status or contact support.

If your order is taking longer than expected:

  1. Check your order status in the "Status" section
  2. Ensure you entered the correct details (phone number, game ID, etc.)
  3. Verify your payment was successful
  4. Contact our support team with your order ID

Most delays are resolved within 30 minutes of reporting.

The transaction ID is crucial because:

  • It verifies your payment was received
  • It helps us track your order quickly
  • It's required for order processing
  • It's used for customer support inquiries

Always save your transaction ID until your order is completed.

Order cancellation depends on the order status:

  • Pending: Can be cancelled with full refund
  • Processing: May be cancelled with partial refund
  • Completed: Cannot be cancelled

Contact support immediately if you need to cancel an order. Refunds are processed within 24 hours.

Your user ID is automatically generated when you first visit our website and is stored in your browser. This helps us:

  • Track your orders efficiently
  • Provide personalized service
  • Maintain your order history
  • Generate your unique avatar

Your user ID remains the same unless you clear your browser data.

If you clear your browser data:

  • Your user ID will be reset
  • Your order history will still be available via order IDs
  • Your theme preference will be reset
  • A new avatar will be generated

You can still track old orders using your order IDs and transaction IDs.

You can contact our support team through:

  • Email: support@easyrecharge.mm
  • Telegram: @EasyRechargeMM_Support
  • Phone: 09-123-456-789 (9AM-9PM)

When contacting support, please provide:

  1. Your order ID
  2. Transaction ID
  3. Service type
  4. Detailed description of the issue

Our refund policy ensures fair treatment for all customers:

  • Full refund: If service is not delivered within promised time
  • Partial refund: If service is partially delivered
  • No refund: For completed services or user errors

Refund requests must be made within 7 days of order placement. Contact support with your order details to initiate a refund.
